About Me

My name is Roman Navarro. I'm from Stockton, California, and I am currently a Sophmore at Stanislaus State University. I am passionate about programming and frontend development -everything you see in my portfolio I taught myself to make through youtube videos and Freecodecamp.com. I am currently studying algorithms and Big-O notation to help me in cracking the coding interview.

On my free time I practice programming (2-4 hours a day), reading, and video games. I am currently employed as a freelance landscape architect and maintenance technician. Certain skills I've acquired from this job that I feel will translate over nicely to my future career as a frontend developer include:

  • discipline -waking up every morning consistently at 7, including on my days off
  • mental endurance -working for long periods of time without getting tired
  • resourcefulness -using what tools I have at my disposal to resolve unlikey tasks
  • clear, concise communication -very important so that everyone is on the same page
